What is Air Pollution?

Air pollution is the presence of harmful substances in the atmosphere that can cause health problems and environmental damage.

Types of Pollutants

  • Particulate Matter (PM)
  • Carbon Monoxide (CO)
  • Sulfur Dioxide (SO2)
  • Nitrogen Oxides (NOx)
  • Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs)
